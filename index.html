About Taylor Damien

Trained at the Stella Adler Conservatory, I’m an actor and performer dedicated to bringing stories to life through stage and screen. My work spans acting, writing, and producing, with a focus on creating authentic, engaging narratives. I’ve performed in diverse settings, from theaters to film sets, and I’m passionate about connecting with audiences through humor, depth, and movement-based performance.

My experience includes collaborations with directors, writers, and performers across various projects, from solo shows to short films. I’m always exploring new ways to tell stories that resonate and inspire.
