Damien Boykin is an Austin-based actor with training from the Stella Adler Studio of Acting 3-Year Professional Conservatory in NYC. Before that, he studied improv at iO and Annoyance Theatre's in Chicago while performing around the city. He has a passion for both stage and screen. He is known for his versatility in dramatic and comedic roles ranging from Shakespeare to Contemporary, to his self-produced comedy shows.

(SAG-AFTRA-Eligible/EMC)
